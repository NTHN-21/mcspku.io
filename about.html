About Us

Mal Ciputra Seraya is one of the biggest attractions found in the city of Pekanbaru, Riau. With over a decade’s worth of history, Mal Ciputra Seraya has stood the test of time and consistently persevered as a relevant shopping mall with its wide range of products and services as well as its excellent and spotless facilities, creating a pleasing atmosphere and a sight for sore eyes. From clothing to dining, and even to recreation, our mall has got all your needs covered and then some. 

Mall Ciputra Seraya is a hotspot for events brought to you by a variety of fresh guests, making every visit to the mall feel different and new. Our spacious interior is complimented by escalators and a lift at the very center, greatly reducing the amount of time it takes to get from one point of the mall to the other. Every corner is brightly lit, that along with the organization of where the attractions have been mapped out makes locations pop out and memorable, ensuring a smooth second visit.

Location: Jl. Riau No.58, Kp. Baru, Kec. Senapelan, Kota Pekanbaru, Riau 28154, Indonesia

Mall Ciputra Seraya is located in Riau st. putting it right alongside loads of busy stores and close by residential areas. Our mall is only a walk away from quite a selection of supermarkets and famous restaurant chains, to compensate for this, our mall is equipped with a parking lot stretching far and wide, guaranteeing open spaces to park your vehicles even during busy hours.
